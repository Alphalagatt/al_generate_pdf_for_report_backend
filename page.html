1. Introduction

This report is the result of a visual inspection of the property at the above address. The inspection was carried out in accordance with the Australian Standard AS4349.1-2007 Inspection of Buildings. The purpose of the inspection was to identify major defects, safety hazards and minor defects in the property. The inspection was limited to those areas of the property that were accessible and visible at the time of the inspection. The inspection did not include areas that were concealed or obstructed, such as wall cavities, roof spaces, sub-floor areas, or areas that were not within the scope of the inspection. The inspection was carried out by a qualified building inspector who is licensed to carry out building inspections in the state of New South Wales.

2. Scope of Inspection

The inspection covered the following areas of the property:

  • Exterior of the building
  • Interior of the building
  • Roof space
  • Sub-floor space
  • Site and grounds

3. Major Defects

The following major defects were identified during the inspection:

  • Cracks in the brickwork
  • Leaking roof
  • Structural damage

4. Safety Hazards

The following safety hazards were identified during the inspection:

  • Exposed wiring
  • Uneven stairs
  • Missing handrails

5. Minor Defects

The following minor defects were identified during the inspection:

  • Cracked tiles
  • Leaking taps
  • Peeling paint

6. Recommendations

It is recommended that the following repairs and maintenance be carried out:

  • Repair cracks in the brickwork
  • Fix leaking roof
  • Replace damaged structural elements
  • Secure exposed wiring
  • Install handrails on stairs
  • Replace cracked tiles
  • Fix leaking taps
  • Repaint peeling paint

7. Conclusion

The property is in need of repairs and maintenance to address the major defects, safety hazards and minor defects that were identified during the inspection. It is recommended that these repairs and maintenance be carried out as soon as possible to ensure the safety and structural integrity of the property.
